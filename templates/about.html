MEET JASON



Jason Hull is a software engineer, artist, and musician, and lives in Portland Oregon with his wife, Michelle, his daughter Maddie, his son Sebastian and his three cats, Poppy, Leo, and Fluffy. He began his professional working career in graphic design and print production. Even at an early age he has always enjoyed the various styles of art. Recently, he has started working in Software Development as a way to integrate the things he enjoys while helping other artists find a place to showcase their work.
